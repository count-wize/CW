Our Mission and Vision:
Honest On-Chain Forensics for UK Fraud Victims

Our Mission
We provide on-chain tracing and written intelligence reports for UK victims of crypto theft and fraud. Our work gives you an accurate, evidence-based picture of where funds moved — a foundation for reporting to police, your bank, or a civil solicitor.
Our Vision
To become the UK's most trusted source of on-chain forensic evidence for fraud victims — a firm known for telling the truth about what the blockchain shows, including when the evidence is not what a client hoped to find.

Core Values
Guiding CountWize

Our values reflect our commitment to honest, rigorous on-chain forensics — telling clients what the blockchain shows, not what they want to hear.
Excellence:
"Delivering accurate, verifiable on-chain intelligence — no inflated claims, no invented outcomes."
Integrity:
"Telling clients what the blockchain shows, including when the answer is not what they hoped for."
Transparency:
"Every report we produce explains its methodology so the recipient can judge its evidential weight."
Innovation:
"Using current forensic tooling to trace transactions across wallets, mixers, and exchanges."
Accountability:
"Taking responsibility for the accuracy of every intelligence report we issue."
Collaboration:
"Working with the client, their solicitor, and where relevant, law enforcement — not instead of them."

Experts Who Make
Crypto Recovery Easy

CountWize was set up after its team watched UK fraud victims lose time — and sometimes more money — by turning to services that made promises the blockchain could not support.
