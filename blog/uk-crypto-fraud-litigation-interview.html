UK Crypto-Fraud Litigation

A UK law firm partner explains how civil crypto-fraud cases work in England and Wales — from emergency freezing orders to on-chain asset tracing and what victims should know before taking legal action.

Crypto fraud is now one of the fastest-growing areas of civil litigation in England and Wales. High Court judges have granted freezing orders over wallets, exchanges have been compelled to disclose account holders, and victims have traced funds across dozens of blockchain hops before identifying defendants. We asked at to explain how this process actually works — and what victims should understand before they consider legal action.
