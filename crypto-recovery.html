Frequently Asked Questions

What does CountWize do?
CountWize provides educational content and on-chain tracing services. We produce a written intelligence report that maps where your crypto went, suitable for police, bank, or solicitor use.
Is CountWize FCA-authorised?
No. CountWize Ltd is not FCA-authorised. We do not provide financial advice, legal advice, or a guarantee of asset recovery.
Can you trace transactions across multiple blockchains?
Yes. Our on-chain tracing covers major blockchains including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others. We map the transaction flow across wallets and exchanges and document our findings in a time-stamped intelligence report.
What does the intelligence report include?
The report is a written, time-stamped document that traces the movement of funds across wallets and exchanges. It is formatted for use with Action Fraud, bank section 75 claims, or civil litigation proceedings.
Do you guarantee recovery of my crypto?
No. We cannot guarantee that tracing results will lead to recovery. Recovery outcomes depend on the specific circumstances of each case. We provide the intelligence report; recovery itself depends on the actions of law enforcement, banks, or courts.
How do I get started?
Request a free 15-minute case review using the form below or on our contact page. We will discuss your situation and explain whether on-chain tracing is likely to be useful in your case.
How do you protect my data?
Your case is confidential. We use encryption for data at rest and in transit, and we never share your information with third parties without your consent. See our Privacy Policy for details.
